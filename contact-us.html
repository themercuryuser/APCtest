Who are American Publication Consultants?

American Publication Consultants Publishers is a publishing house based in the United States with branches globally. We are a renowned publisher of scholarly research in Science & Technology, Engineering Sciences and Management Sciences with globally standardized practices and a transparent publishing process.

What is the process of publishing at American Publication Consultants?

Our publishing process is similar, and in some instances more transparent, to that of our publishing partners. Our processes are approved by our indexing and quality partners, Scopus, and COPE. To get more information about the publishing process, please consult our publishing work-flow.

Why should I choose American Publication Consultants?

American Publication Consultants Publishers offer it authors a timely and in-situ view of the publishing process. With our Open Access policy and adherences to the Budapest Open Access Initiative, we offer competent documenting, indexing, archiving, editing and dissemination services in accordance with global standards.

What can I do if I cannot afford the Article Publishing Charges (APC)?

American Publication Consultants Publishers commitment to the research community is stringent. If you cannot afford the standard APCs, please avail our Funding Services for Authors to get intimated on how we can help you out. We envision a world where every authentic research should be published, and we have prompted initiatives to achieve that.

How is my published research indexed and archived?

American Publication Consultants Publishers reaffirms its commitment to serve the research community by providing indexing in all major indexing bodies (Scopus, DRJI, DOAJ, ISI and SJIF). Your published research will be digitally archived and preserved in CLOCKS.

What is Open Access Policy (OA)?

With the advent of the BOAI, Open Access publishing of scholarly research has been the norm. Open Access Policy refers to the transparent practice, in according with COPE principles, of publishing your research to readers without subscription. This ensures that your research is timely disseminated and accessible to a wider global audience
